Common Questions

Is this a form of therapy?
No. Somnora is a wellness tool designed for reflection and journaling. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or clinical therapy.
Do I have to log my dreams every night?
Not at all. Somnora is optional. You can use it as a simple sleep tracker or only engage when you have something on your mind.
How does HealthKit factor in?
We pull sleep duration and stage data (REM, Deep, Core) to help Nora frame its observations around your physical rest.
Can I export my data?
Yes. You can export your entire journaling history as a structured file at any time.
How does Nora adjust its tone?
Nora adapts between reflective, structured, and calm modes depending on context, while staying short-first and non-clinical.
