Frequently Asked Questions

Is my dream data private?

Absolutely. Somnora uses 100% on-device AI processing. Your dreams are encrypted locally and never sent to cloud servers. Optional cloud sync (2026) uses end-to-end encryption.

What devices work with Somnora?

iPhone (iOS 16+), Apple Watch (Series 3+), and any third-party sleep tracker that syncs with Apple Health. No expensive hardware required.

When can I get Somnora?

TestFlight beta: December 2025 (early access waitlist)
Public iOS launch: Q1 2026
Android version: Q3 2026

How much does it cost?

Free tier with basic features. Premium is $5/month ($50/year). Early access users get 50% off first year. Lifetime membership available for $299.

What makes Nora different from ChatGPT?

Nora is purpose-built for therapeutic dream exploration. She asks thoughtful questions that promote self-discovery rather than giving interpretations. She learns your personal sleep patterns over time, connects insights to your HealthKit data, and maintains conversational context across sessions. Most importantly, she's designed to be a gentle companion‚Äînot a know-it-all.

What AI technology powers Somnora?

Somnora uses a hybrid AI architecture. Core analysis runs on-device using Apple's Core ML for privacy. For deeper conversations with Nora, we use Google Cloud's Vertex AI with enterprise-grade security. Your raw dream content is never stored on external servers‚Äîonly anonymized insights are processed.

How accurate is the sleep tracking?

Somnora reads from Apple HealthKit, which aggregates data from your devices. Apple Watch offers research-validated sleep stage detection comparable to professional sleep labs.
