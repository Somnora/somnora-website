Account Deletion

Last Updated: June 29, 2026

Delete from the app: SquadSync includes an in-app Delete Account option in Settings. This page explains that flow and how to request deletion by email if you need to.

Delete From the App

To permanently delete your account from within SquadSync:

  1. Open SquadSync.
  2. Go to Profile or Settings.
  3. Open the account/settings area.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Read the confirmations and confirm only if you want to permanently delete the account.

After server confirmation, the app signs you out. Relaunching the app will require fresh authentication.

If You Do Not See Delete Account

If Delete Account is not visible in your app version, email support@squadsync.app with the subject "Squad Sync account deletion request."

Include the app-visible display name for the account and any safe information that helps us identify it. Do not send your Apple ID password, private tokens, or unnecessary private messages. We may ask you to confirm control of the account before deletion.

What Deletion Does

  • revokes active sessions where supported;
  • signs the app out after deletion is confirmed;
  • deactivates notification/device tokens where supported;
  • removes or scrubs profile-identifying fields;
  • stops the deleted account from using protected account endpoints.

Some shared group history may remain with a generic deleted-user placeholder where needed to preserve squad, session, chat, report, or moderation context for other users.

What Deletion Does Not Do

Deletion does not remove content from other users' devices if they already received it, and it may not remove records we must keep for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, safety, dispute resolution, or service integrity.

Need Help?

Contact support@squadsync.app. See also our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
