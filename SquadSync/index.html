Everything Your Crew Needs

From planning game night to finding one more, keep your squad on the same page.

CORE FEATURE

Plan Game Night in Seconds

Create a session, send it to your squad, and watch the RSVPs roll in. See who's going, who's running late, and add it to your calendar so nobody misses the drop — turning "who's on tonight?" into a clear plan.

Squads Your Way

Build permanent squads for your regular crew or temporary ones for a single night. Share scoped invite codes that can expire, limit uses, and be revoked when plans change.

Squad Chat & DMs

Keep the plan and the conversation in one place with squad chat and friends-only direct messages — no noisy servers or unrelated channels to dig through.

Find / LFG

Need one more for the squad? Post a Find request when your team needs another player, then keep the group moving from request to session.

Always in the Loop

See what's coming up next right from Home, add home-screen widgets for your next session, and get push reminders so you never miss game night.
