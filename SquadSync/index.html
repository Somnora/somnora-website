SquadSync

Level Up Your Teamwork

SquadSync is the ultimate coordination tool for gaming communities, designed to bring players together and keep the action going.

Core Features

Squad Management

Create permanent squads for your regular group or join new ones. Manage members, roles, and squad settings with ease.

LFG (Looking For Group)

Find players for a specific game or session. Post your availability and browse requests from others in the community.

Integrated Chat

Stay connected with your squad through real-time messaging, emoji reactions, and media sharing.

Session Planning

Schedule gaming sessions, manage RSVPs, and use "Ready Checks" to ensure everyone is set to play.

Reputation System

Build your status in the community through positive interactions and consistent session attendance.

Cross-Platform Sync

Access your squads and messages across iOS, Apple Watch, and more, keeping you in the loop wherever you are.

Safety First

We believe in building a positive gaming environment. SquadSync includes robust safety features, including user blocking, reporting tools, and privacy controls to ensure you stay in command of your social experience.
