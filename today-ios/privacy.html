Today In Time

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 10, 2026

Privacy First: Today In Time is a "this day in history" app designed with your privacy as a priority. Everything stays on your device. We don't collect, store, sell, or have access to your personal information, and the app makes no network requests for analytics or tracking.

1. Information We Collect

Short answer: None.

Today In Time does NOT collect or transmit:

  • Personal identification information
  • Email addresses or account credentials (the app has no accounts)
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or fingerprints
  • Crash reports tied to your identity

2. Data Stored On Your Device

The App stores a small amount of preference data locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults system. None of this is transmitted off-device.

Data Type What It Is Where It Lives
Tone preference Whether you prefer Uplifting or Balanced events Your device only
Reading density Compact, Standard, or Comfortable layout Your device only
Reactions Thumbs up / thumbs down you give to events Your device only

This data never leaves your device. It is used solely to remember your settings and reactions between sessions.

3. Historical Event Content

The historical events shown in the app are bundled inside the App itself. The App does not need an internet connection to display events, and it does not call any servers (operated by us or by anyone else) to fetch, log, or report what you read.

4. Home Screen Widget

The Today In Time widget displays a single event from the same bundled content on your home screen. The widget reads the same local data as the App, performs no network requests, and does not collect information about you.

5. Device Permissions

Today In Time does not request any device permissions. It does not access:

  • Camera or Photo Library
  • Microphone
  • Location Services
  • Contacts or Calendar
  • Health, Motion, or other sensor data
  • Bluetooth or local network

6. Third-Party Services

Today In Time does NOT integrate:

  • Google Analytics, Firebase, or any analytics SDK
  • Facebook SDK or any social SDK
  • Advertising networks or ad SDKs
  • Third-party crash reporting that collects user data
  • Any third-party tracking technology

The only platform services involved are Apple's own iOS APIs (e.g., UserDefaults, WidgetKit), which run on your device.

7. App Tracking Transparency

Today In Time does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies. Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt is therefore not shown, and the App's privacy manifest declares no tracking and no collected data types.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

Because all of your data is local, you control its lifetime entirely:

  1. To clear reactions and preferences: delete the Today In Time app from your device. iOS will remove the App's local data.
  2. Reinstalling the App starts you fresh with default settings.

Since we never receive your data, there is nothing for us to delete on our end.

9. Children's Privacy

Today In Time does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The App contains no advertising, no accounts, and no communication features.

10. International Users

Your preferences and reactions stay on your device. Because we do not receive your data, no international data transfer takes place.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion of personal information, and opt out of the sale of personal information.

Our compliance: We do not collect, sell, or share personal information. We will never sell your data.

12. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

EU/EEA residents have rights including access, rectification, erasure, and portability of personal data. Because we do not collect or process your personal data, these rights are satisfied by default. Your data remains entirely under your control on your own device.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. If a future version of the App begins collecting any data, we will update this Policy and surface the change inside the App before that collection begins.
