Public Beta Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated March 6, 2026.

Somnora is built for private sleep reflection. We keep collection narrow, use data to generate useful context, and avoid turning your journal into an advertising asset.

What We Collect

  • Sleep duration, sleep stages, and timing data you choose to share through Apple HealthKit.
  • Journal entries, dream notes, and other reflections you enter directly in the product.
  • Basic contact or account details needed for access, billing support, and service communication.

How We Use It

  • To generate sleep summaries, pattern observations, and calm next-step prompts inside Somnora.
  • To maintain your account, support troubleshooting, and improve product reliability during beta.
  • To operate privacy-first AI workflows without selling private journaling content.

Your Controls

  • You can export or delete your data, your reflections are not locked into the product.
  • We do not use private entries to train public-facing AI models.
  • HealthKit data stays permission-based, and you can revoke access from Apple Health at any time.

Contact

Questions about privacy or data handling can be sent to privacy@somnora.app.
