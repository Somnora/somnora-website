FAQ

  • Is this financial advice? No. Cosmo Trader is informational and entertainment only, not financial, investment, legal, tax, or trading advice.
  • Does Cosmo Trader trade for me? No. The app does not execute trades, connect to brokerage accounts, custody assets, or move money.
  • Why does market data differ from my broker? Market data may be delayed and comes from third-party providers, so it may differ from the data shown by your broker or another provider.
  • How do I manage Oracle Tier? Use Apple subscriptions from your Apple Account settings or the Manage subscriptions link above.
  • How do I delete/reset my app data? Use Profile > Privacy & Data in the app to reset implemented local profile, portfolio, watchlist, preferences, and usage counter data.
