Cosmo Trader

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 16, 2026.

Cosmo Trader is an entertainment and market-watching app from Somnora that combines stock market data with astrology and zodiac framing. This policy explains what information the app may use, how it is stored, and how to contact us.

Product Scope

Cosmo Trader is informational and entertainment only. It is not financial advice, investment advice, legal advice, tax advice, trading advice, or a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold any security.

Cosmo Trader does not execute trades, connect to brokerage accounts, custody assets, or move money. You are responsible for your own financial decisions and should consult qualified professionals where appropriate.

Information You Provide or Generate

  • Birth date or zodiac-related profile inputs used to personalize app framing.
  • Watchlist symbols and raw search queries you enter while using market-watching features.
  • Manually entered portfolio holdings, if you choose to record them in the app.
  • Notification preferences, local app preferences, and usage counters.
  • Subscription status and entitlement state from Apple StoreKit.
  • Support requests and the contact information you choose to include when you email us.

Market Data

Stock and market data is provided through third-party market-data providers such as Finnhub. Market data may be delayed, incomplete, unavailable, or different from the data displayed by your broker or another provider. We do not claim that Cosmo Trader provides real-time or no-delay market data.

Storage and Services

Much of the app data is stored locally on your device, including profile details, watchlists, manually entered holdings, preferences, and counters where implemented. Some app functionality may call Somnora backend services for profile sync, referrals, inbox or daily briefing features, push registration, authentication, or app health depending on the app version and configuration.

Some builds use FirebaseCore and FirebaseAuth for app setup or authentication flows. Cosmo Trader does not use Mixpanel or a third-party crash reporting service to profile your activity.

Analytics and Privacy Posture

  • Cosmo Trader is not designed to track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • Raw search queries, watchlists, and portfolio contents are not sold.
  • Current app analytics are local-only and used for app behavior such as counters, limits, and preferences.
  • We do not add advertising trackers or tracking scripts to this website.

Purchases and Subscriptions

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple StoreKit. Somnora receives purchase status and entitlement state so the app can unlock paid features, but we do not receive your full payment card details.

You can manage Apple subscriptions at https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, Cosmo Trader may use your notification preferences and, for push notifications, device tokens to deliver alerts, daily briefings, or app messages. You can change notification permissions in iOS Settings.

Deletion and Reset

Cosmo Trader includes in-app deletion or reset controls intended to clear implemented local profile, portfolio, watchlist, preferences, and usage counter data. You can also delete the app from your device to remove its local app data.

Deleting or resetting app data does not delete every record held by Apple, including App Store purchase, subscription, or transaction records that Apple maintains under its own policies.

Children's Privacy

Cosmo Trader is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information to us, contact us so we can review the request.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as Cosmo Trader changes. When we do, we will update the effective date above. Material changes may also be surfaced in the app or on this website.

Contact

Cosmo Trader is operated by Somnora. For support, visit the Cosmo Trader support page or email support@somnora.app.
