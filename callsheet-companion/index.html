Callsheet Companion

A personal record-keeping app built for film and TV crew workflows.

Support

For help, questions, or feedback, email support@somnora.com.

What it does

  • Log shoot days, call times, wrap times, and rates
  • Track earnings, overtime estimates, and mileage for your own records
  • Organize crew contacts and follow-ups
  • Keep gear inventory and rental history
  • Store paystubs and sensitive documents in a Face ID-protected Vault on device

App data is stored on your device and, when you are signed in to iCloud, synced to your own iCloud account. The developer does not collect or transmit your data.

Callsheet Companion is a personal record-keeping tool. Calculations are estimates based on the information you enter and are not official payroll, accounting, or tax advice. Verify pay, overtime, penalties, and tax treatment with your production, payroll company, or a qualified professional.
